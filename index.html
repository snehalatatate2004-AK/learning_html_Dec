Vitthal Mandir is a very famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vitthal (Vithoba), who is believed to be an incarnation of Lord Krishna.

This holy temple is situated in Pandharpur, Maharashtra, on the banks of the Chandrabhaga (Bhima) River.

The idol of Lord Vitthal is made of black stone and stands on a brick with hands on hips, symbolizing patience and devotion.
