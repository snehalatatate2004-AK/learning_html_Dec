Vitthal Mandir

Vitthal Mandir, Pandharpur

Vitthal Mandir is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vitthal (Vithoba), an incarnation of Lord Krishna. It is located in Pandharpur, Maharashtra.

Spiritual Importance

The temple is the center of the Bhakti movement. Thousands of devotees called Warkaris visit the temple every year.

Main Attractions

  • Black stone idol of Lord Vitthal
  • Goddess Rukmini temple
  • Chandrabhaga (Bhima) River
  • Ashadhi and Kartiki Wari
